## Data Sources Reference

### Ready to Use (No Setup Required)
- **SEC EDGAR:** https://www.sec.gov/cgi-bin/browse-edgar - Earnings calls, contracts, filings
- **arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/ - Research papers
- **GitHub Public Repos:** Open source PRs and code
- **Kaggle Datasets:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets - Various domains
- **Public Contract Templates:** Google "sample lease agreement PDF", "employment contract template"

### Requires Account/Credentialing
- **MIMIC-III:** https://physionet.org/content/mimiciii/ - Clinical data (requires credentialing)

### Generate Your Own
- Use Claude/GPT to generate synthetic: meeting transcripts, clinical notes, customer support tickets
- Scrape with permission: job postings, real estate listings, news articles
